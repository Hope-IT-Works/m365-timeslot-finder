M365 Timeslot-Finder

Sign in with your Microsoft account to find available time slots for your meetings.

Ready in
three steps.

Configure your search and let the extension do the rest — no more manual calendar comparisons.

1
Set your search period
Pick a start and end date and choose the desired meeting duration (15 min – 8 hours).
2
Select required attendees
Search your organisation's directory by name or email. All selected participants are checked simultaneously.
3
Copy your meeting proposal
Get a list of all free slots and copy a formatted proposal to your clipboard — ready to paste into any email or chat.
